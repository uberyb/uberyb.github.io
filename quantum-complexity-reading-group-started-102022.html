Quantum Complexity reading group (started 10/2022)

Background

This reading group was started by Sanchayan Dutta to study complexity theory. Ultimately, we seek to gain a background in the quantum PCP conjecture. Each week various members of the reading group give a short presentation based on research, what we encounter in the notes, or related topics of interest. Slides from presentations will be uploaded when available and I'll link to some things which were interesting to me that I plan to go back and read about should I stop procrastinating.
