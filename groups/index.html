Quantum complexity student seminar

This is a student lead seminar focused on studying quantum computational complexity. It initially began focusing specifically on the quantum pcp conjecture but has branched out to include other topics in complexity theory of interest to the group members.

Our weekly meetings usually consist of presentations or discussion.

Quantum algebra student seminar

This is similar to the previous group however it has a greater focus on abstract mathematics. This includes operator algebras, quantum algebra, knot theory.

Kim lab

Research group lead by Isaac Kim. Research tends to focus on quantum error correction and noisy intermediate-scale quantum devices/algorithms.
