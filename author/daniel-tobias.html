About me

I'm a PhD student studying computer science at UC Davis with research interests lying in CS theory and quantum computing. I'm advised by Greg Kuperberg. I participate in a few quantum computing groups at Davis, namely Kim Lab and QuIST which is made up of many wonderful physicists, mathematicians, computer scientists, and engineers at UC Davis.

Previously I was a Master's student of computer science at Davis, and before that I did my undergraduate in pure mathematics at Towson University under the advisement of Angel Kumchev.
