Quantum Complexity reading group (10/2022)

Background

This reading group was started by Sanchayan Dutta to study complexity theory. Ultimately, we seek to gain a background in the quantum PCP conjecture. Each week various members of the reading group give a short presentation based on research, what we encounter in the notes, or related topics of interest. Slides from presentations will be uploaded when available, and on occasion I plan to add notes about the meeting which could vary from something expository to quick links to new things I've learned.
